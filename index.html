The scoring model

Why RICE, not gut feel

Every theme gets four honest inputs. The formula keeps loud-but-low-impact requests from jumping the queue, and gives stakeholders a number to argue with instead of opinions.

R

Reach

How many users this touches per quarter.

I

Impact

How much it moves the metric, per user.

C

Confidence

How much evidence backs the estimate.

E

Effort

Person-weeks to build and ship it.

RICE = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) ÷ Effort  →  higher score, higher in the roadmap
